My Golf Training and Performance Page

Golf has taught me discipline, patience, and the value of constant improvement. This page highlights some of the things I enjoy most about the game.

Why I Love Golf

Golf is a sport that challenges both the body and the mind. It requires focus, confidence, and consistency.
I have played golf since I was 7 years old. It has been a huge part of my life as it has taught me very valuable lessons. It has taught me how to be patient, how to work hard, and how to never give up. Golf is a game of constant improvement, and I love the challenge of trying to get better every time I step on the course.

My Practice Routine

  1. Warm Up
    • Stretch
    • Hit short wedges
    • Practice putting
  2. Full Swing Practice
    • Work on alignment
    • Focus on tempo
    • Hit a variety of clubs
  3. Short Game Work
    • Chipping drills
    • Bunker practice
    • Putting drills
